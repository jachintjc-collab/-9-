Capcom Pattern Recognition

Capcom's history of RE main titles follows clear thematic cycles: Action-focused (RE5/6) → Horror-focused (RE7) → Hybrid (RE8) → Classic feel (RE2-RE4 Remakes, RE9). RE10 will likely continue the classic feel or pivot to a new direction. Both options have community precedent and corporate sense.
