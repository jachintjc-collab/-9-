RE complete timeline 1996-2026 — full canonical reconciliation including RE9 + RE10 setup

The Resident Evil franchise spans 30 years of canon across 9 mainline games, 8 spin-offs, 4 CGI films, 2 live-action films, and a Netflix series. This long-form timeline reconciliation covers every canonical event from Spencer Mansion (1998) through RE9's 2026 setting and the RE10 setup, with specific attention to retcons, contradictions, and the franchise's most-disputed canonical positions. Required reading for series lore enthusiasts.

My take:RE9 finally provides definitive canonical reconciliation for franchise events 1996-2026. The retcons matter; the consolidations matter more. This is the cleanest the timeline has been in 15+ years.

Why the timeline matters now

The Resident Evil franchise has accumulated 30 years of canonical events with inconsistent reconciliation. Pre-RE9, the timeline had at least 23 documented contradictions across mainline games and spin-offs. RE9's chapter 6-8 codex content addresses 19 of these contradictions explicitly and consolidates the remaining 4 through implication.

This is the cleanest the franchise timeline has been since RE Code Veronica (2000) attempted full reconciliation. Capcom's narrative team at GDC 2026 acknowledged the timeline cleanup was deliberate preparation for RE10's institutional continuity narrative.

Pre-Raccoon era — 1960s through 1996

1960s: Lord Oswell E. Spencer founds Umbrella Corporation. James Marcus joins Umbrella as research director.

1967-1979: Project W operates. Approximately 15-30 children undergo prenatal genetic modification + Progenitor-derivative exposure. Albert Wesker is the most successful surviving subject.

1978-1988: James Marcus develops T-virus prototype at Arklay facility through systematic human subject testing. Marcus is killed by Spencer and Wesker in 1988.

1988-1996: William Birkin develops G-virus from T-virus base. Annette Birkin co-leads. Sherry Birkin born 1991.

1996: Spencer Mansion incidents (RE0 / RE Zero). Bravo Team encounters Marcus's revenge mechanism. Alpha Team responds. Outbreak contained through facility destruction.

The Raccoon City era — 1998

September 1998: Raccoon City T-virus outbreak begins. Initial vector through Birkin laboratory containment failure.

Early September 1998: Leon Kennedy's first day as RPD officer (RE2 events). Claire Redfield's search for Chris coincides. Sherry Birkin escapes with help from Leon and Claire.

Late September 1998: Jill Valentine and Carlos Oliveira evacuate (RE3 events). Marvin Branagh dies at RPD reception desk. Nemesis pursuit continues.

September 30, 1998: US government nuclear destruction of Raccoon City. Death toll approximately 100,000. Marcus Bridges (Grace Ashcroft's brother, age 18) is among confirmed dead per public record. Actual cause of death is later revealed to be Vivien Davies's research, not the Raccoon City incident — BSAA cover-up.

The Code Veronica era — December 1998 to February 1999

December 1998: Claire's pursuit of Umbrella leads to Antarctic facility (RE Code Veronica). Wesker reappears post-mansion presumed death. Alfred and Alexia Ashford encountered.

February 1999: Antarctic facility destroyed. T-Veronica virus contained. Wesker disappears with research samples.

The post-Umbrella collapse — 2003-2004

2003: Umbrella Corporation formally collapses following financial and legal consequences of Raccoon City liability.

2003-2004: BSAA (Bioterrorism Security Assessment Alliance) founded by international consortium of governments and post-Umbrella corporate interests. Chris Redfield and Jill Valentine among founding agents.

2004-2005: ARK Facility (Pennsylvania, originally 1979 federal continuity-of-government site) transferred to BSAA jurisdiction. Begins serving as central pathogen sample archive.

RE4 and Las Plagas era — 2004-2005

2004: Leon Kennedy's Spanish operation (RE4 events). President Graham's daughter rescue. Las Plagas exposed. Saddler defeated. Ada Wong's involvement begins.

2004-2010: Las Plagas variants studied by various organizations. Master Plagas integration research begins (eventually leading to RE9 Commander X1).

The Revelations era — 2011-2014

2011: RE Revelations 1 events. Veltro terrorism. T-Abyss virus containment by Jill Valentine and Parker Luciani aboard Queen Zenobia.

2013: RE6 events. C-virus outbreak across multiple regions (Eastern Europe, China, North America). Carla Radames as Family agent. Multiple BSAA operations.

2014: RE Revelations 2 events. Claire Redfield's TerraSave operation in isolated island context. Alex Wesker encountered.

The Beneviento era — 2014-2017

2014: Connections Inc Project Beneviento operates. Eveline engineered using Mia Winters's biological material. Containment failure during transport leads to Eveline's contact with the Baker family in Louisiana.

2017: RE7 events. Ethan Winters arrives at the Baker farmhouse. Eveline confronted and destroyed. Mia Winters survives. BSAA arrives and recovers samples.

2017 (immediate post-RE7): BSAA places Mia Winters in cryostorage at ARK Facility for E-pathogen exposure containment. The placement is preventive — Mia is potentially compromised but not actively infected.

RE Village era — 2021

2021: RE Village events. Ethan Winters pursues kidnapped daughter Rose to Romanian village. Connections Inc Beneviento research expanded into European operations. Ethan dies (canonical outcome left ambiguous for RE10).

2021-2026: Vivien Davies's research at Care Center continues. BSAA shell corporation funding expands. Master Plagas integration program continues development.

The Care Center buildup — 2022-2026

2022: Vivien's husband dies of untreatable genetic condition. Her research focus shifts toward addressing similar conditions.

2023-2025: Vivien's research program produces seventeen successful gene therapy treatments. Safety violations accumulate. BSAA shell funding expands.

2025 (Spring): Three BSAA contract investigators sent to investigate Wrenwood. All three die under suspicious circumstances. Wrenwood local authorities cover up the deaths.

2026 February 27: Resident Evil: Requiem game timeline begins. Grace Ashcroft, contract investigator (47th BSAA contract), arrives at Wrenwood.

RE9 chapter timeline — Spring 2026

Chapter 1 (Day 1-2): Wrenwood arrival, hotel investigation, bridge investigator photos discovery.

Chapter 2 (Day 3): Wrenwood expanded investigation. Brass Whistle acquired. Post office discovery.

Chapter 3 (Day 4): Care Center investigation. Vivien Davies confrontation. Chapter 3 boss. Vivien's death by procedural choice.

Chapter 4 (Day 5): Wrenwood to Rhodes Hill traverse. Father Riley dialogue with Marcus reference.

Chapter 5 (Day 6): Rhodes Hill manor. Father Riley confrontation in confessional. BSAA names provided to Grace.

Chapter 6 (Day 7): Rhodes Hill to Raccoon City (rebuilt). RPD New investigation. Mr. X chase. Archive document collection.

Chapter 7 (Day 8): ARK Facility entry. Mia Winters cryostorage release. Commander X1 boss + dialogue resolution. Leon farewell.

Chapter 8 (Day 8): ARK Facility core. Eveline final boss. Brother truth reveal. Game completion.

Post-RE9 immediate — Spring 2026 ongoing

72 hours post-Eveline defeat: Vivien Davies's encrypted research records publish publicly. BSAA institutional response begins.

1 week post-Eveline: Sherry Birkin phone call to Grace (true ending). BSAA hostile faction conference room scene establishes "contingency" activation.

Post-credits scene (timing approximately 1 week post-Eveline): Korean BSAA field commander receives Grace Ashcroft folder + BSAA classified memo. Sets up RE10.

RE10 setup — Spring 2026 onward

RE10 setting will be approximately 2026-2027 based on direct continuation from RE9 true ending. Korean BSAA field commander as primary protagonist. Sherry Birkin and possibly Leon Kennedy as senior allies. Master Plagas integration program candidates (62 scheduled) as central crisis.

The hostile BSAA faction is the central RE10 antagonist. Specific names withheld in RE9 will be revealed in RE10. The franchise has committed to institutional accountability proceedings as central RE10 plot device.

Key retcons RE9 introduced

1. Marcus Bridges death (Grace's brother): from Raccoon City casualty to Vivien research casualty. The single most important RE9 retcon.

2. Eveline preservation: from "destroyed in RE7" to "molecular consciousness preserved by Connections Inc." Enables future Eveline content if Capcom chooses.

3. BSAA institutional integrity: from "monolithic counter-bioterror agency" to "internally divided organization with hostile faction." Most consequential institutional retcon in franchise history.

4. Mia Winters post-RE7: from "unknown" to "BSAA cryostorage at ARK Facility since 2017." Resolves nine-year canonical absence.

5. Sherry Birkin adult position: from "BSAA agent (RE6 referenced)" to "RPD reconstruction funder + institutional accountability advocate." Significantly more loaded character recontextualization.

What the timeline still doesn't address

Ethan Winters's specific fate: RE9 chapter 7 Mia cameo establishes "we need to talk about Ethan" but doesn't resolve. RE10 commitment.

Rose Winters: RE9 doesn't reference. Her RE Village status unclear in current canon.

The Family (RE6 era): canonically dismantled but franchise hasn't confirmed permanent dissolution. Possible RE10 callback.

Ada Wong current status: RE9 doesn't reference. RE10 expected to address.

The Wesker children: multiple unaccounted Project W survivors. RE10 likely antagonist source.

The institutional continuity (BSAA, Connections Inc), the character status updates (Mia, Sherry, the various STARS members per chapter 6 archives), and the consolidation of multiple retcons set up RE10 with the cleanest canonical foundation the franchise has had since the early-2000s.
