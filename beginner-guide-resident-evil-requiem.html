Beginner guide to Resident Evil: Requiem (RE9)

Comprehensive beginner overview of RE9. What to expect, how to start, where to invest first playthrough attention.

My take:RE9 is the franchise's most-narrative game. First playthrough rewards story engagement over tactical optimization.

What kind of game

Tactical horror investigation. Combat-heavy. Lore-dense.

Recommended approach

Standard difficulty. Read codex. Complete optional content.

Time investment

16-18 hours for first playthrough.

Save strategy

Use 6 slots. Cloud backup on Insanity.

What to avoid first playthrough

Spoilers. Speedrunning. Skipping cutscenes.

My take

RE9 is best experienced slowly first. Speedrun later.
