RE9 confirmed Vivien's E-virus was bred in Bulgaria. Eastern Europe is uncharted RE territory. Capcom's love of rural Gothic horror (RE8 Romania-inspired) makes this 45% likely.

West African Plateau, Wesker Children 30% probability

RE5 was in Africa. Wesker's children appearance in RE9 DLC and Albert Wesker resurrection rumors point toward an Africa return.

US East Coast, ARK Boston Branch 20% probability

ARK facility in RE9 references a Boston branch. American-set RE games (RE2/RE3 Raccoon, RE6 Tall Oaks) sell well.

Japan — Capcom's Home Turf 5% probability
