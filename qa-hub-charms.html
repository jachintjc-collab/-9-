10개 질문 답변

Q: How many charms can I equip?
A: 4 simultaneously.
Q: Where are all 18 charms?
A: See our All 18 Charms Tier List for locations.
Q: Best charm overall?
A: Phoenix Down — revives once per chapter. Universal.
Q: Worst charm overall?
A: Lucky Star — RNG-dependent, low impact.
Q: Charms in NG+?
A: Carry over fully. Cap at 4 equipped (no change from Standard).
Q: Stackable charms?
A: No. Each charm slot is unique.
Q: Knife Edge — required for knife-only?
A: Highly recommended. +40% knife crit damage.
Q: Eye Spy Charm — what does it do?
A: Reveals hidden items within 5m radius. Niche utility.
Q: Hidden 19th charm?
A: Rumored. Datamined as 'Eternity' but not confirmed in v1.02.
Q: Charm trading?
A: No. Single-player only.
