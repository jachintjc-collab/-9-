Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MAY 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how re9guide.it.com ("we", "us", or "the site") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website at www.re9guide.it.com.

Information we collect

re9guide.it.com is primarily a static content site. We collect minimal personal information directly. The information we may collect includes:

// AUTOMATIC INFORMATION

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (where you came from)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level only)
  • IP address (anonymized for analytics purposes)

// INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

  • Email address — only if you contact us via our contact email
  • Message content — only what you choose to send us

How we use information

We use collected information for:

  • Improving guide quality and identifying popular topics
  • Understanding which devices and browsers our visitors use
  • Responding to messages sent via our contact email
  • Detecting and preventing site abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

re9guide.it.com may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences (such as theme)
  • Measure anonymous traffic patterns
  • Enable third-party services like analytics and advertising

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some site functionality but will not prevent you from reading guides.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

// GOOGLE ANALYTICS

We may use Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior in aggregate. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data about page views, session duration, and traffic sources. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

// GOOGLE ADSENSE

If we display advertisements, we may use Google AdSense. Google AdSense uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on prior visits to our site or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

// GITHUB PAGES

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect access logs as described in the GitHub Privacy Statement.

// KO-FI

If you choose to support the site through Ko-fi, your transaction is processed by Ko-fi Lab Limited. Their privacy policy applies to that transaction.

Data retention

Email correspondence is retained for as long as needed to address your inquiry. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics default retention settings (typically 14-26 months).

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Right to access — request what data we have about you
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of your data
  • Right to correction — request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Right to opt-out — opt out of analytics and personalized advertising

To exercise any of these rights, email re9uiem@re9guide.it.com.

Children's privacy

re9guide.it.com is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

International users

re9guide.it.com is operated from South Korea. If you are visiting from outside South Korea, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws than your own.

For EU/EEA visitors (GDPR): By using this site, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. The legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (operating and improving the site) and consent (for analytics and advertising cookies).
For California visitors (CCPA): California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Significant changes will be announced on our home page.
