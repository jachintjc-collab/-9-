10 questions answered

Q: How do I beat Mr. X without dying?
A: Stay mobile + use Grenade Launcher stagger. Library route skips entire chase.
Q: Vivien Stage 2 keeps killing me. what charm?
A: Mortal Edge + Knife Edge charm. Frame-perfect parry counters.
Q: Father Riley boss tips?
A: Magnum to chest, dodge the cleric charge. Reset patterns at 50% HP.
Q: Nurse Kim — is the basement fight optional?
A: No, required. Use Stun Gun + knife. Save before entering.
Q: Commander X1 multi-phase. best charm?
A: Iron Skin + Phoenix Down. Phase 3 has tightest dodge windows.
Q: Eveline final boss; Phase 4 strategy?
A: Counter the grapple with frame-perfect parry. Use Hand Cannon.
Q: Wrenwood Wraith on Insanity?
A: Watch left elbow for phase tell. 6-frame dodge window.
Q: Plant 43 — fire vs explosive?
A: Fire (Flame Rounds) faster. Explosive better for resource economy.
Q: Titan Spinner mini-boss, required?
A: Optional but rewards Rocket Launcher (NG+ carry-over).
Q: Hardest boss in game?
A: Insanity NG+ Eveline Phase 4. ~6% completion rate.
