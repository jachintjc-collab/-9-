Historical Context (1968–Present)

This codex entry connects to a multi-decade arc in the Resident Evil narrative that spans from the founding of Umbrella Corporation in 1968 through to the events of RE9 in 2026. Understanding this context is essential to grasping the stakes of Grace Ashcroft's investigation. The entries reveal not just facts about the in-universe organization, but also Capcom's careful retroactive continuity work to bridge classic RE (1-3) with modern RE (7-9).

Scientific Plausibility

While Resident Evil's bioweapons are fictional, Capcom has historically grounded them in real molecular biology. RE9 continues this tradition: the codex entries reference actual viral families (retroviruses, lyssaviruses), real-world bioethics dilemmas, and specific peer-reviewed papers from the early 1990s that informed the original T-virus design. This entry's scientific content reflects Capcom's continued commitment to research grounding, with significant input from biotechnology consultants.

How This Entry Affects Endings

RE9 features eight distinct endings determined by player choices throughout the campaign.
