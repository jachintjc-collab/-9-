About RERequiem.guide

RERequiem.guide is an independent, fan-made guide site dedicated to Resident Evil: Requiem (RE9). Every guide is hand-written based on real gameplay testing — no recycled content, no filler, no AI-generated fluff.

Why this site exists

When Resident Evil: Requiem launched on February 27, 2026, I started searching for guides like every other player. What I found was scattered — some sites had safe codes but missed the Insanity-difficulty variants. Others had boss strategies but skipped the optional bosses with the best item drops. A few had collectibles lists but missed the missable ones that disappear after story progression.

So I decided to write the guides I wished existed: clear, complete, spoiler-controlled, and tested in real playthroughs. Every safe code in this guide has been verified. Every Mr. Raccoon location has been double-checked. Every boss strategy includes both Standard and Insanity difficulty notes.

What you'll find here

  • Boss guides — full attack patterns, weak points, and Insanity-difficulty tips for all 8 main bosses + 2 optional encounters
  • Safe codes — all 5 safes with both Normal and Insanity combinations
  • Collectibles — exact locations for all 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam, 22 Antique Coins, 75 Files, and 9 Plant Seedlings
  • Puzzle solutions — Quartz Puzzle Boxes, Blood Specimen Analyzer, Roulette Wheel, and the Final Puzzle
  • Weapon stats — full database of all 17 weapons with tier rankings and upgrade priorities
  • Trophy guides — paths for all 49 trophies including Platinum strategy
  • Beginner tips — spoiler-free guides for new players

Who runs this site

Hi, I'm Jachin. I'm a longtime Resident Evil fan based in Seoul, South Korea. RE2 Remake hooked me on the series back in 2019, and I've platinumed every mainline RE game since. Requiem felt different enough — and big enough — to deserve its own dedicated guide site.

I run this site solo, on my own time. No team, no sponsor, no Capcom involvement. Just one player writing guides for other players.

Editorial principles

1. Hand-written from real gameplay. Every guide is based on my own playthroughs across multiple difficulties. I cite community consensus where my own testing is incomplete.

2. No AI-generated filler. AI tools may help me organize notes or check grammar, but the strategies, locations, and analysis come from actual playtime.

3. Spoiler control. Boss guides are clearly labeled with spoiler warnings. The Beginner's Guide is 100% spoiler-free.

4. Updated regularly. Capcom's patches and player community discoveries change strategies. I revisit guides monthly to keep them accurate.

5. Free forever. Core guides will never be paywalled.
