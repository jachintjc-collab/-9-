Insanity Conqueror — complete the game on Insanity difficulty

Complete RE9 from chapter 1 through Eveline final boss on Insanity difficulty. Trophy unlocks at credits. Estimated 25-40 hours focused playthrough. NG+ allowed but not required.

My take:Insanity Conqueror is the franchise's standard hardcore trophy. Required for the harder challenge trophies. Pursue once for the achievement, then move on.

What unlocks it

Complete the game on Insanity difficulty setting. The difficulty must be set to Insanity at chapter 1 start (or higher; no difficulty above Insanity exists in RE9). Lower-difficulty start that's later changed to Insanity doesn't count.

NG+ Insanity counts. First-playthrough Insanity counts. Specific save state requirements: must complete on a single Insanity-difficulty save.

The Insanity difficulty modifications

Enemy HP +75% over Standard. Enemy damage +50% over Standard. Reduced ammo drops. Reduced medication drops. No auto-aim. No tutorial pop-ups. Save scumming required for survival in most encounters.

Most dangerous Insanity encounters

Chapter 3 Vivien Plant 43 boss. The boss can one-shot if you don't have Endurance Charm equipped.

Chapter 6 Mr. X. One-shot on contact. Routing must be perfect.

Chapter 7 Commander X1. Phase 3 has multiple one-shot attacks. Frame-perfect parry required.

Chapter 8 Eveline phase 2 tendril sweep. One-shot if positioned in sweep path.

Required Charm loadout for Insanity

Endurance (essential — converts some one-shots to survivable damage). Marksman's Eye (essential — crit damage compensates for reduced ammo). Quickstep (recommended — faster dodge frames). Mortal Edge (NG+ only — knife combat).

Korean Insanity strategy community

Korean RE9 Insanity strategy threads on Naver are some of the most active Korean RE community content. The Korean Insanity-specific tactics for boss fights are documented with frame-precise timing.

My take

Insanity Conqueror is the gateway to higher trophy challenges (True Survivor, knife-only Insanity, etc.). Pursue once. Use Korean Naver tactics for boss fights. The completion itself is the reward; don't expect the trophy notification to feel special.
