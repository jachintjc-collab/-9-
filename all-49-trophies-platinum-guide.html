Trophy overview

Resident Evil Requiem features a balanced trophy list with mix of story, combat, collectible, and challenge achievements. Most trophies (~30) unlock naturally through normal play. The remaining 19 require specific actions, collectibles, or difficulty completions.

Key facts every trophy hunter should know:

  • Infinite Ammo cheats do NOT void trophies. The Special Content Store unlocks Infinite Requiem and Infinite RPG after first completion — both are safe to use on Insanity.
  • You only need to play through twice. One run on any difficulty (for collectibles + Insanity unlock) and one Insanity run.
  • Several trophies are missable. See the Missable Warning section below before starting.
  • Estimated total time: 30-40 hours including Insanity speedrun and 100% collectibles.

⚠ Missable trophies warning

Critical missables — pay attention to these:

1. Eye Spy — Chunk disappears after Grace's section ends
2. Order Up! — The Chef must be killed during Grace's chapter
3. You Little Rascal! — Several Mr. Raccoons are area-locked
4. Case Closed! — Many files are story-locked, can't backtrack
5. Coin Collector — Some Antique Coin sources are missable
💡 Save strategy: Use ALL 12 manual save slots. Save at the start of every area. If you miss a collectible, you can reload to that exact point. There is no chapter select after credits — your saves are your only safety net.
