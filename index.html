Frequently asked questions
What is Resident Evil: Requiem (RE9)?+

Resident Evil: Requiem is the ninth mainline entry in Capcom's Resident Evil series, released February 27, 2026 on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, Nintendo Switch 2, and PC (Steam and Epic Games Store). It stars FBI agent Grace Ashcroft and returning veteran Leon S. Kennedy in a return to Raccoon City. The game holds a Metacritic score of 88 — the best mainline RE reception in over 20 years.

Is re9guide.it.com an official Capcom site?+

No. re9guide.it.com is an independent fan-made guide site, not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Capcom. All trademarks, character names, and game content are the property of Capcom Co., Ltd. The .it.com domain reflects this — it's an honest signal that this is a small fan project, not a major media outlet.

Are these guides spoiler-free?+

Each guide is labeled with a 3-tier spoiler rating (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH). The Beginner's Guide and weapon tier lists are spoiler-controlled for first-time players. Boss strategy pages and the Platinum trophy roadmap necessarily contain late-game spoilers, marked clearly. See our Spoiler Policy page for full details.

How often is the site updated?+

Guides are updated weekly, with major patch coverage same-day when Capcom releases new content. Each guide displays its LAST VERIFIED date and the GAME VERSION it was tested on. v1.02 (May 2026 patch) is the current verified version across all live guides.

Can I use these guides on Insanity difficulty?+

Yes. Every boss guide and walkthrough on this site includes Insanity-specific tips, damage multipliers, parry-window changes, and recommended Infinite Ammo strategies. Insanity is unlocked after one campaign clear on any difficulty (including Casual). Cheats including Infinite Ammo do NOT void Insanity-related trophies.

Who writes the guides on this site?+

All guides are hand-written by Jachin, a longtime Resident Evil fan based in Seoul, South Korea. Every strategy is based on real playthroughs across multiple difficulties on PS5 and Steam. AI tools may help organize notes or check grammar, but strategies, locations, and analysis come from actual playtime — see the About page for full editorial principles.
