All 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam Locations — Resident Evil Requiem

All 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam locations in Resident Evil Requiem, organized by area in story order. Destroying all 25 unlocks the "You Little Rascal!" trophy and the Advanced Tuning upgrade tier for Leon's weapons (Tier 3 max upgrades).

Quick reference

Total: 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam statues

How to destroy: Knife, gunshot, or grenade — all work. Knife is free.

Reward: "You Little Rascal!" trophy + Advanced Tuning (Tier 3 weapon upgrades)

Bonus: Some are missable. Once you leave an area, you cannot return.

⚠ Important: Several Mr. Raccoons are character-locked. Some can only be reached by Grace, others only by Leon. Look for the character tag on each entry. Wear headphones — Memoriams emit a faint high-pitched chime when you're within ~5 meters.
Jump to area
  • #1-9 Care Center (Rhodes Hill)
  • #10-22 Raccoon City
  • #23-25 ARK Facility

Care Center — Rhodes Hill // #1-9

The Rhodes Hill Care Center is the largest area in the game and contains the most Mr. Raccoons. Grab the Raccoon Roundup map from the Workshop safe (basement) using code R60-L40-R80 — it marks all Care Center Memoriams on your map.

#01
Fireplace mantel — Rehabilitation WardLEON
After Leon defeats the chainsaw maniac and zombie patients in the Rehabilitation Ward, look on the fireplace mantel in the hallway just south of the ward. The first Mr. Raccoon in the game. Has an attached note reading: "Hunt us, seek us! Find us, break us!" Unmissable — also unlocks the "The Hunt Begins" trophy.
#02
East Wing Lobby — Reception deskGRACE
Sitting on top of the receptionist's desk in the East Wing Lobby. Out in the open — easy to spot when you walk through.
#03
East Wing Stairwell — Table by grandfather clockGRACE
East of the Waiting Room, in the stairwell on a small table beside the grandfather clock. Easy to miss if you head straight upstairs.
#04
Kitchenette — Coffee machineBOTH
In the Kitchenette connected to the West Wing Office. On top of the coffee machine on the second floor.
#05
Medication Room — Locked cell beside Emily'sGRACE
Inside the Level 3-locked cell adjacent to Emily's room. Behind the bed in the empty cell. Requires Level 3 wristband obtained from the Isolation Ward sequence.
Missable if you progress past the Isolation Ward without backtracking.
#06
Records Room (2F) — Warped cabinetLEON
Inside a cabinet with a warped door on the second floor. Only Leon can pry open warped cabinets with his hatchet — Grace cannot reach this one.
#07
Basement Bunkroom — On top of TVGRACE
In the cells beneath the Care Center, sitting on top of the television in the Bunkroom. Unmissable when you sweep the basement cells.
#08
Basement Workshop — On workbenchGRACE
Sitting on the workbench in the Basement Workshop. Access via the vent opposite the basement forklift, or by powering the door from the holding cells.
Same room as the Workshop Safe (R60-L40-R80) — open it for the Raccoon Roundup map.
#09
Private Lab — Victor's office deskGRACE
In Victor's house Private Lab while searching for the helicopter key. On the desk near the Helicopter Keys pickup. Last chance before transitioning to Leon's chapter — destroy it before triggering the cutscene.
Highly missable — easy to miss during the tense escape sequence.

Raccoon City // #10-22

Raccoon City is the largest combat zone with Mr. Raccoons spread across rooftops, sewers, and inside buildings. You'll need a sniper rifle (Marksman 1A from BSAA Container 03) for several long-distance shots in this area. A Raccoon Roundup map for Raccoon City is purchasable from the bonus menu post-story for a few hundred CP.

#10
Front gate pedestal — Sniping support sequenceLEON
During the first sniper support sequence where Leon defends Grace and Emily on their way to the church. Aim down your scope, look to the left of the gate near a light fixture. You'll see a moving object on a pedestal — that's the Mr. Raccoon. Shoot it with the sniper rifle.
If you miss it during sniping, you can sometimes return to ground level before entering the chapel. Grace cannot reach this earlier due to obstacle blocking.
#11
Cafe Oasis — Pine cabinet behind counterLEON
Inside Cafe Oasis on the route toward the Applegate Hotel. The Mr. Raccoon is in the pine cabinet behind the counter / bar area. If you miss it, you can return through the subway later.
#12
Underground Parking Garage — Side room cratesLEON
After visiting BSAA Central Camp, head into the Underground Parking Garage. In the side room (near the stealth kill prompt area), sitting on top of large plastic-wrapped crates.
#13
Bus interior — Driver's side windowLEON
From Central Camp, cross to the other side of the gap. There's a burned-out bus on the road north of Cedarbrook Apartments near the Gas Powered Generator. The Mr. Raccoon sits in the bus window on the driver's side.
#14
Broken sewer pipe — In the road holeLEON
From the bus, continue forward. Use the crane near the gap to drop cargo and form a bridge. Once on the bridge vantage point, look down into the hole below — the Mr. Raccoon is on a broken sewer pipe at the bottom. Shoot from above. Can also be reached later from inside the sewer pipes near Ridgewood Station.
#15
Highway overpass — On top of a vanLEON
From the same bridge position, tilt your view nearly straight up. A van is parked on the overpass — the Mr. Raccoon is on top of the van. Snipe it from below by lining up the angle. Alternative shot: from Cedarbrook Apartments rooftop during the detonator search, facing west.
Tricky shot — bring the Marksman 1A or any scoped weapon.
#16
Gas Station — Shelf opposite front doorLEON
Inside the Gas Station in East Raccoon City, on the shelf directly opposite the front door. Sweep the interior, not just the pumps outside.
#17
Subway car / Train — On the seatLEON
On your way to Ridgewood Station, head west into the subway car. The Mr. Raccoon sits on a seat on the north side of the train. Spiders spawn in this area — that's your indicator you're in the right spot.
#18
Willis Tower — Side room shelvingLEON
While moving through Willis Tower toward the first detonator section. After the first staircase, before continuing south (the correct path), head east (right) into the room at the end of the corridor. The Mr. Raccoon is on the shelving unit.
#19
Cedarbrook Apartments — Inside open fridgeLEON
After using the rusty crank/handle to progress in Cedarbrook Apartments. In the corridor area, look for an open fridge — the Mr. Raccoon is inside.
#20
Cedarbrook Stairwell — After ladderLEON
After using the rusty crank on the gate on the balcony area, head up the ladder, take a right, and go inside the stairwell. The Mr. Raccoon is on the stairwell wall/ledge.
#21
Logistics Warehouse — Roof, on vanLEON
Once you've picked up the Disruptor from the Logistics Warehouse Roof, do a 180 and stand to the right of the elevator. Look directly ahead to the highway — the Mr. Raccoon is on the roof of a vehicle in front of a small lorry.
#22
Apartment window — Behind Gun Shop KendoLEON
In the alleyway behind Gun Shop Kendo, follow it to the end and look up. The Mr. Raccoon is in an apartment window to the left of the large Umbrella Corporation billboard.
💡 Bonus #22b — RPD Library (some guides count this separately): Inside the Raccoon City Police Station library, behind the large central bookcase. Hidden behind a pile of books. Some collectible counters list this as #23 instead of the Kendo alley shot.

ARK Facility // #23-25

The final area. Only 3 Mr. Raccoons here, but easy to miss in the rush toward the final boss. Sweep every room before the point of no return — once you trigger the Victor Gideon cutscene, you cannot return.

#23
Staff Room hallway — Behind cratesLEON
From the first ARK save room (the Staff Room), head south. Just before you reach the steps leading to the Service Corridor, turn around and look behind the crates and cage you just walked past. The Mr. Raccoon is hidden behind them.
#24
First ARK hallway — On boxes behind trolleysLEON
In the first hallway of the ARK Facility, head to the end and turn right. The Mr. Raccoon sits on top of a box behind some hospital trolleys. Easy to miss if you don't sweep the corner.
#25
Operations Room — Computer consoleGRACE
The final Mr. Raccoon. Reach the Operations Room as Grace — the same room where you obtain the Noblesse Orb key item. The figure is on top of the computer console near the massive monitors on the southern wall. Watch out for Lickers in this area.
Last chance for 100% — break this before triggering the final boss cutscene.

Rewards for finding all 25

What you unlock

1. "You Little Rascal!" trophy — Silver. Auto-unlocks on the 25th destruction.

2. Advanced Tuning — Permanently unlocks Tier 3 weapon upgrades for all Leon weapons at Supply Boxes. This is the highest upgrade tier in the game and required for the "Fully Loaded" trophy.

3. CP rewards — Each Mr. Raccoon destroyed grants Challenge Points. Spend at the Special Content Store on Infinite Ammo and the RPG-7.

4. "The Hunt Begins" trophy — Bronze. Unlocks on the very first Mr. Raccoon destruction (#1).

Tips for finding them faster

🎧 Wear headphones. Each Memoriam emits a faint high-pitched chime when you're within about 5 meters. On TV speakers, it's easy to miss entirely.
🗺️ Get the Raccoon Roundup map first. The Workshop Safe in the Care Center basement (code R60-L40-R80) contains a map that highlights all Care Center Mr. Raccoons on your map. A separate Raccoon City map is purchasable from the bonus menu post-story.
📷 Check the Records menu regularly. The collectibles tracker shows your progress. If you finish an area and the count is below the expected total, return immediately before the point of no return.
💀 Destroyed counts persist across deaths. If you break one and die before reaching a save point, it still counts as destroyed. Confirmed on PS5 and PC.
