TL;DR — what to collect first

  • Top priority (don't miss): Eveline's Locket + Master Key + Antique Coin. These three define your endgame.
  • Trophy-required (Platinum hunt): Family Photograph + Quartz Fragment Set + Silver Pendant. Missing any = no Platinum.
  • NG+ unlocks: Master Key + Silver Pendant + ARK Security Badge — only available in NG+ runs.
  • Skip on first playthrough: Tarnished Necklace, Rusty Spoon, Worn Notebook. Just sell them.

S-tier — must-collect items

#1: Eveline's Locket (Lore + Insanity unlock)

Why #1: The most lore-significant item in RE9. Hidden in ARK Facility Vivien lab. Opening it unlocks the Eveline-Vivien connection codex (the franchise's most important lore reveal in years). On Insanity, possessing this locket triggers Eveline's whisper audio during boss fights. See our Eveline complete profile.

#2: Master Key (Universal door access — NG+ only)

Why #2: NG+ exclusive. Opens every locked door in the game. Unlocks 7 previously-inaccessible rooms across all chapters. Essential for the "Completionist" trophy and exposes the 3 hidden lore documents not findable on first playthrough.

A-tier — high-value items

#3: Antique Coin (Currency × 10 rare)

Why A-tier: The single most valuable currency item. 6 total Antique Coins exist in the game (3 in Chapter 5, 3 in Chapter 8). Collecting all 6 = 60,000 in-game currency, enough to buy 3 tier-3 weapon upgrades or 4 high-tier charms.

#4: Family Photograph (Vivien lore reveal)

Why A-tier: Found in Care Center records office. Shows Vivien Ashcroft holding a child labeled "E—" (Eveline). This is the smoking gun connecting Grace to Eveline. Trophy-required (Platinum).

#5: Quartz Fragment Set (Puzzle progression)

Why A-tier: Required for the Quartz puzzle. 5 fragments scattered across chapters. Completing the puzzle unlocks the Insanity-only Eveline reference photo and the Chunk family audio.

#6: Silver Pendant (NG+ charm slot unlock)

Why A-tier: Hidden in Chapter 6 orphanage attic. Equipping this unlocks the 4th charm slot in NG+. Massive build flexibility upgrade — see our charms tier list for optimal 4-charm loadouts.

B-tier — solid items

#7-11: Keys and badges

Bronze Key, RPD Master Key, Care Center Pass, ARK Security Badge — all gateway items that unlock chapter-specific rooms. The Brass Whistle is unique: throwing it generates noise that distracts enemies. Useful on Insanity stealth segments.

C-tier — lore-only items

#12-15: Lore tokens

Vintage Pen, Old Diary, Stained Photo, Ceramic Doll — these unlock codex entries but provide minimal gameplay value. Worth collecting for lore completionists but skippable on speedruns.

D-tier — skippable items

#16-18: Junk items

Tarnished Necklace, Rusty Spoon, Worn Notebook — sell to vendor for 100-500 currency each. No lore value, no gameplay impact. Rusty Spoon is a reference to RE7's starting inventory (easter egg only).

Items required for Platinum trophy

To achieve Platinum, the following items must be collected:

  • Family Photograph (Chapter 2) — "Vivien's Truth" trophy
  • All 5 Quartz Fragments — "Mosaic" trophy
  • Eveline's Locket (Chapter 8) — "The Catalyst" trophy
  • All 6 Antique Coins — "Treasure Hunter" trophy
  • Silver Pendant + Master Key (NG+ only) — "Completionist" trophy

See our NG+ complete guide for the optimal collection order across 3 playthroughs.

NG+ exclusive item drops

Three items only appear in NG+ runs:

  1. Master Key — automatically given at NG+ start
  2. Silver Pendant 2nd location — appears in Chapter 8 (different from first-run location)
  3. Eveline's Reference Photo — Insanity NG+ only, in Vivien lab

FAQ — item collection

Q: Can I collect all 18 items in one playthrough?
A: No. Master Key, Silver Pendant 2nd location, and Eveline's Reference Photo are NG+ exclusive. Minimum 2 playthroughs (1 standard + 1 NG+) required for 100% collection.

Q: Are items missable?
A: Yes — most are missable if you skip optional rooms. The exceptions are Eveline's Locket (mandatory pickup in Chapter 8) and Quartz Fragments (required for puzzle progression).

Q: Which item is hardest to find?
A: Antique Coin #6 in Chapter 8. Hidden behind a secret panel in the ARK Facility executive office. Requires the ARK Security Badge to access the office first.

Q: Do items respawn in NG+?
A: No. Items collected in NG don't respawn — but NG+ adds 3 new items not present in the first run.

Q: What's the best item to sell?
A: Vintage Pen (1,500 currency) and Old Diary (1,200). Both have low lore weight and good resale. Skip selling Antique Coins — keep them as currency multipliers.

Q: Are there hidden items not on this list?
A: The Insanity-only Eveline Reference Photo isn't a standard item — it's a special unlock. Beyond that, no hidden items exist.

Q: Can items break or be lost?
A: No. Items persist in inventory across all playthroughs. Collecting one in NG carries to NG+ as "owned."

Q: Will RE10 keep this collection system?
A: Director Nakanishi confirmed collection systems will return. Form unconfirmed.
