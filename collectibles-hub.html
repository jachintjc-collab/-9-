Collectibles FAQ
How many collectibles are in Resident Evil Requiem?
There are 5 main categories: 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam, 75 Files, 22 Antique Coins, 5 Locked Safes, and 9 Plant Seedlings. Total 136 individual items across the game.
Which collectibles are missable?
Many collectibles are missable due to point-of-no-return transitions. The most critical missables include Mr. Raccoon #5, ARK-area Files (#70-73), and the optional boss-locked Pantry Inventory File. Use manual saves before each major area transition.
Do I need 100% collectibles for Platinum?
Yes for some, no for others. Required: Case Closed (75 Files), You Little Rascal (25 Mr. Raccoons), and No Safe Is Safe (5 safes). Easier: Coin Collector only needs one Antique Coin spent.
Can I collect everything in one playthrough?
Yes if you're careful. Use this hub's location guides + save before every major transition. If you miss something, NG+ retains your weapons and unlocks but resets collectibles.
Which character collects what?
Grace-only: Antique Coins, Safe codes, NG+ Final Puzzle items. Both characters: Mr. Raccoon Memoriams, Files. Leon-only: Most Raccoon City and ARK area collectibles.
