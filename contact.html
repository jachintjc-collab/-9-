What I can help with

  • Guide corrections — incorrect codes, missing locations, outdated strategies
  • Content suggestions — guides you'd like to see written
  • Partnership inquiries — collaborations, content sharing, media outreach
  • Technical issues — broken links, page errors, mobile display problems

What I can't help with

  • Game support — bugs, crashes, save corruption (contact Capcom support)
  • Account issues — PSN, Xbox Live, Steam (contact the platform)
  • Spoiler-heavy questions in public — if your question has spoilers, mention "SPOILER" in the subject line
  • Personal coaching — I don't offer 1-on-1 gameplay help, but I'm happy to expand a guide based on your question

Response times

Typical response: 24-48 hours.
Slower periods: First weeks after a major Capcom patch (community feedback floods in).
If you don't hear back in 5 days, resend the message — emails sometimes get filtered.

About this site

re9guide.it.com is run by a single person — me, Jachin — based in Seoul, South Korea. This is a hobby project, not a company. Response times reflect that. I appreciate your patience.
