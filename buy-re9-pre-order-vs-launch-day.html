Detailed analysis

Pre-order incentives vs launch reviews. The decision depends on:

  • How quickly you want to play: Immediate = Standard. Patient = wait for sales.
  • How long you keep games: Long-term keeper = digital + Deluxe. Try-then-resell = physical Standard.
  • Region/currency: Korean PSN often cheapest; check exchange rate.
  • Subscription status: If already PS Plus Premium, day-one if included = saves $60.
  • DLC interest: If you'll play DLC season = Deluxe saves ~$15 vs separate buy.

When to buy

Buy immediately if:

  • You're a long-time RE fan (hype matters)
  • You'll play through quickly (under 2 weeks)
  • You want to discuss with the community at launch

Wait if:

  • You're new to RE — first impression matters; wait for patches
  • You're price-sensitive — Q4 2026 will see Steam discounts
  • You're unsure about difficulty (try demo first)
