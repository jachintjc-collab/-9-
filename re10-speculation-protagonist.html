Grace Ashcroft's Daughter (Age 19 — 2028) 65% probability

RE9 epilogue shows Grace's daughter as a young teen. Capcom's pattern of 'character ages with player' (RE2 to RE6 Leon, RE7 to RE9 Ethan/Rose) suggests a time skip. 9-year gap to RE10 release (~2029) puts her at 19 — perfect protagonist age.

Leon S. Kennedy's Final Mission 20% probability

Leon's RE9 ending hints at one more deployment. Capcom rarely retires Leon mid-arc. RE10 could be his swan song with an apprentice handoff.

Chris Redfield's Last Stand 10% probability

Chris was teased in DLC as 'on assignment.' BSAA dissolution arc could see Chris in RE10 as a war-weary commander.

Brand New Character 5% probability

Capcom resets protagonists every 2-3 games (Ethan in RE7 was new). RE10 could introduce a third-generation BSAA member tied to RPD/Raccoon City heritage.

Capcom Pattern Recognition

Capcom's history of RE main titles follows clear thematic cycles: Action-focused (RE5/6) → Horror-focused (RE7) → Hybrid (RE8) → Classic feel (RE2-RE4 Remakes, RE9). RE10 will likely continue the classic feel or pivot to a new direction. Both options have community precedent and corporate sense.
