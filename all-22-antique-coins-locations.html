All 22 Antique Coins in Resident Evil Requiem — Complete Locations

PUBLISHED MAY 2026 · 10 MIN READ · 100% COLLECTIBLE GUIDE

Antique Coins are the special currency Grace uses at The Parlor in the West Wing. Spending them unlocks weapon attachments, rare items, and crafting materials. There are 22 total coins across the game — find them all and you'll have more than enough to fully stock the Parlor.

How Antique Coins work

Antique Coins are exclusive to Grace's character — Leon cannot pick them up or use them. They serve as the Parlor merchant's primary currency.

  • Spending location: The Parlor (West Wing 1F)
  • Trophy: "Coin Collector" Bronze unlocks on first Parlor purchase
  • Carry-over: Unspent coins do NOT carry over to NG+
  • Total in game: 22 coins, 4-5 more than needed to buy everything

What to spend coins on at the Parlor

The Parlor merchant offers various items in exchange for Antique Coins:

  • Weapon attachments — Scopes, extended mags, suppressors (3-5 coins each)
  • Rare crafting materials — Premium Scrap, High-Quality Blood (2-3 coins each)
  • First Aid Sprays — At a discount vs. crafting (2 coins each)
  • Hemolytic Injectors — Pre-crafted for Grace (3 coins each)
  • Cosmetic items — Outfits and weapon skins for post-game (5-10 coins)
💡 Priority spending: Buy weapon attachments first. The extended magazine for the Blacktail and the suppressor for stealth handgun use are the biggest gameplay improvements.

Missable warnings

⚠ Missable coins: The RPD Library coin (#16) is locked behind the Mr. X chase. If you don't return after defeating Super Tyrant, you can't get it. The Underground Parking Garage coin (#17) requires draining water — if you skip the battery puzzle, you can't reach it.

Coin Collector trophy

The "Coin Collector" Bronze trophy unlocks the moment you spend your first Antique Coin at the Parlor. You don't need to find all 22 — even a single 2-coin Hemolytic Injector purchase triggers it.
