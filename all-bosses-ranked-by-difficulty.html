How this ranking works

Each boss is scored on five factors:

  • Damage output — how hard their hits land
  • Attack pattern complexity — how many moves they have, how readable
  • Parry difficulty — how tight the parry windows are
  • Resource demand — how much ammo/healing the fight consumes
  • Insanity scaling — how punishing they become on the hardest difficulty

The list goes from hardest (#1) to easiest (#8). Order matters: knowing what's coming lets you save resources for the right fights.

S Tier — Brutal fights

#1
Dr. Victor Gideon
// ARK FACILITY · FINAL
★★★★★
The hardest fight in Resident Evil Requiem. Two-phase Nemesis-clone with electric attacks, RPG-7, tentacle sweeps, and a 10-second purple charge window. Mortal Edge Hatchet is mandatory. Don't waste ammo before this fight.
CANON ENDING 2-PHASE LEON
#2
The Commander (HUNK-type)
// ARK CORRIDORS
★★★★★
Knife-fights like Krauser from RE4. Fast attacks with readable tells. The most skill-intensive fight in the game. Reward: Mortal Edge Hatchet — required for Victor.
PARRY-FOCUSED LEON
#3
Mr. X — Super Tyrant
// OUTSIDE RPD
★★★★★
Iconic boss with thrown cars and leap attacks. Aim for the heart — body shots do almost no damage. Two-phase with chase sequence first.
CHASE+FIGHT LEON HEART WEAKPOINT

A Tier — Challenging fights

#4
Titan Spinner
// RACCOON CITY PLAZA
★★★★★
Massive spinning bioweapon. Cannot parry its spin attacks — you must use cover. Burst damage triggers temporary stun windows. Save Requiem rounds for stuns.
COVER-BASED LEON
#5
Plant 43
// ARK ENTRANCE
★★★★★
Three-headed plant monstrosity. Shoot side buds first, then central flower when its maw opens. Finisher: shoot explosive canisters from the dislodged truck for an instant cinematic kill.
CINEMATIC FINISHER LEON

B Tier — Manageable fights

#6
Chunk #2 (Timothy B. Jackson)
// CARE CENTER ATTIC
★★★★★
Second Chunk encounter. Pick up the MSBG 500 Shotgun immediately at fight start — it's beside the corpse. Use yellow ladders to force pathing.
SHOTGUN PICKUP LEON
#7
Chunk #1 (Thomas K. Jackson)
// CARE CENTER EAST WING
★★★★★
Optional but recommended. Slow mutant that snags on doorframes. 3× Hemolytic Injector from behind = instant kill. Drops Eye Spy Charm.
OPTIONAL GRACE CHARM REWARD
#8
The Chef
// WEST WING KITCHEN
★★★★★
Easiest "boss" in the game. Optional encounter — drops Pantry Key for free supplies. One Hemolytic Injector instant-kills him. Easy to skip if speedrunning.
OPTIONAL GRACE/LEON

Boss order in the campaign

For story planning, here's the order you'll fight bosses:

  1. The Chef (Care Center Kitchen) — optional, very early
  2. Chunk #1 (East Wing) — optional, recommended for Eye Spy Charm
  3. Chunk #2 (Care Center Attic) — mandatory mid-game
  4. Mr. X / Super Tyrant (Outside RPD) — mandatory, post-Care Center
  5. Titan Spinner (Raccoon City Plaza) — mandatory mid-Raccoon City
  6. Plant 43 (ARK Entrance) — mandatory, early ARK
  7. The Commander (ARK Corridors) — mandatory, mid ARK
  8. Victor Gideon (ARK Final Chamber) — final boss

Pre-fight prep checklist

Universal pre-boss preparation:

  • Save manually before the fight (use a fresh slot)
  • Repair body armor at the nearest Supply Box
  • Stock 2+ First Aid Sprays
  • Reload all weapons before triggering the cutscene
  • Equip the strongest melee weapon (Mortal Edge Hatchet ideally)
  • Consider equipping Eye Spy Charm (random fatal damage save) for Insanity
