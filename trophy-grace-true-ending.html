Grace True Ending — unlock the true ending route

The true ending requires specific dialogue choices and complete chapter 6 archive reading. Trophy unlocks at the post-credits scene (the Korean BSAA field commander scene). Most players miss this without a guide.

My take:Grace True Ending is the franchise's most narratively important trophy. Required watching for anyone planning to play RE10. The unlock conditions are obscure but the payoff is significant.

The unlock conditions

Read all chapter 6 RPD archive documents (specifically the Sherry Birkin involvement files and the Marvin Branagh case file). Choose the third dialogue option ("I don't accept this") during the chapter 8 Eveline final boss conversation. Complete the boss fight.

Both conditions are necessary. Missing either fails the true ending and gives the standard ending instead.

The chapter 6 archive details

Five specific documents must be read: "Sherry Birkin — BSAA personnel file" (chapter 6 main archive), "Reconstruction memo 2010" (same archive), "Marvin Branagh case file 1998" (RPD chief's office), "Investigator deaths summary 2025-2026" (chapter 6 secondary archive), "Brother case file 1998-0127" (chapter 6 deep archive — requires manor study key from chapter 5).

The brother case file is the hardest to access. It requires the manor study key from chapter 5 (acquired by solving the manor study puzzle). Most players skip the puzzle and lock themselves out of true ending without realizing.

The dialogue choice details

During the Eveline boss fight, Eveline initiates three dialogue exchanges. The third exchange (mid-fight, after phase 2 begins) offers three response options: "I had to" (default, standard ending), "I'm sorry too" (alternative standard), "I don't accept this" (true ending requirement).

The third option only appears if you've read all five chapter 6 archive documents. Missing the documents removes the option from the dialogue menu — most players see only two options and don't realize a third was supposed to be available.

Verifying you're on track

The pause menu's archive section shows read documents. If all five are marked read, you're eligible for the true ending dialogue option. Check before the chapter 8 boss fight.

If you reach the Eveline fight without all five read, you cannot acquire the third dialogue option on that save. NG+ replay required.

The true ending content

Three additional minutes beyond the standard ending. Sherry Birkin phone call. BSAA conference room scene with hostile faction. "TO BE CONTINUED" title card. Confirms RE10 development direction.

Insanity considerations

True ending trophy doesn't require Insanity. Standard difficulty completion with correct conditions produces the trophy. Pursue on Standard if you only want the trophy without the Insanity challenge.

Korean community on true ending

Korean Naver community documented the true ending unlock conditions within 36 hours of launch. The conditions are more thoroughly documented in Korean RE9 guides than in English-language equivalents. Search "RE9 진엔딩 조건" for current Naver coverage.

My take

Grace True Ending is the trophy that distinguishes engaged players from speedrunners. Plan for it. The unlock conditions reward careful chapter 5-6 exploration. The post-credits content is essential for RE10 anticipation.
