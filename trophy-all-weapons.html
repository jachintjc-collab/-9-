All Weapons — 17 weapons, every one needed, two easy to miss

Seventeen weapons in RE9. Possession of all 17 (not just unlock, actual possession) triggers the trophy. Two are easy to miss because their unlock requires specific dialogue choices or non-obvious actions.

My take:All Weapons is the trophy that pays off knowing the game. First-playthrough completion rate is under 5% per Capcom telemetry. Worth understanding why before going for it.

The 17 weapons by category

Handguns (4): Silencer 9, Match Pistol, S&W M1911, Hand Cannon.

Shotguns (3): W870 Police, S&S M232, Ithaca Stakeout.

Rifles (3): Bolt-Action Hunter, Hunter Repeater, Marksman's M40.

Heavy (2): Rocket Launcher, GM-79 Grenade Launcher.

Special (3): Knife, Mortal Edge knife, Flare Gun.

Hidden (2): Quickdraw Army (chapter 5 puzzle reward), Compound Bow (chapter 7 character choice).

The two trophy-locking weapons

Quickdraw Army requires solving the Rhodes Hill manor study puzzle in under 2 minutes. The puzzle has unlimited attempts but the speed requirement is strict. Failing the speed run locks the weapon for that save — NG+ resets.

Compound Bow requires choosing the third dialogue option ("I work alone better") in the chapter 7 Mia Winters cameo scene. The default and second options give you a different bonus (extra ammo). The bow specifically requires the third option.

Insanity unlock difference

Hand Cannon unlocks on Insanity completion (carry-forward to NG+). Mortal Edge knife unlocks on knife-only Insanity completion. Both can be unlocked early through specific NG+ glitches, but Capcom patched most of them in v1.02. As of v1.02, intended difficulty progression is required.

Trophy verification timing

The trophy triggers at the moment of acquiring the seventeenth weapon. If you sold a weapon to a merchant earlier (this is possible with three of the weapons), you need to repurchase before the trophy registers. The system checks current possession, not historical possession.

Korean speedrun community weapons route

Naver RE9 speedrun community has documented the All Weapons category route — a specific playthrough order that acquires all 17 weapons in minimum time. The route requires NG+ start with Hand Cannon and Mortal Edge already unlocked.

My take

All Weapons is the trophy that confirms you understand RE9's systems. Don't attempt on first playthrough — you'll miss the dialogue-locked weapons. NG+ second playthrough with All Weapons in mind is the realistic completion path.
