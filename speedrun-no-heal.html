What makes this category unique

Healing items banned. Mortal Edge parries only. This means specific design choices throughout the route: weapon selection, charm equipping, save management, and dialogue choices all factor differently than a standard playthrough. Endurance charm + Survivor's Will

Chapter-by-chapter route breakdown

The optimal route through No Heal Run category requires careful chapter-by-chapter planning:

Chapter 1: Awakening at Wrenwood (target 30:00)

Open with the tutorial sequence as fast as the game allows. Skip optional dialogue. Pickups: only required items. Use the Silencer 9 starter as designed. Avoid Mr. Raccoon collectibles (unless 100%). Speedrun-critical: the bridge sequence at 28:00 mark has a 4-frame window for the optimal jump.

Chapter 2: Wrenwood Hotel (target 25:00)

Hotel boss requires Charm setup. For Restriction category specifically, the boss strategy is highly optimized. Sub-strategy: aggressive parry-counter usage with Mortal Edge if available; otherwise W870 Police shotgun headshot stagger pattern.

Chapter 3: Care Center Arrival (target 25:00)

Vivien Stage 1: weakness is crit headshot pattern. Use Marksman's Eye charm equipped. Speedrun-specific: avoid the optional research note reading (saves 90 seconds). Speedrunners go directly to the Plant 43 mini-boss area.

Chapter 4: Care Center Confrontation (target 22:00)

Vivien Stage 2 + Nurse Kim boss fights. Frame-perfect Mortal Edge parries are essential. The community has documented exact i-frame windows. For Restriction category, this is where most run-killing deaths happen.

Chapter 5: Rhodes Hill Manor (target 22:00)

Painting puzzle solution: code 560 (well-known). Manor combat is largely optional — speedrunners skip 60% of encounters with strategic running. Rhodes Hill Doctor boss: weakness is knife counter.

Chapter 6: Raccoon City (target 35:00)

RPD section reuses RE2 Remake routing. Mr. X chase: take the alternate route through Library to skip 4 minutes. Titan Spinner: rocket launcher 1-shot if NG+ available.

Chapter 7: ARK Lower (target 22:00)

Final puzzles + Commander X1 phase 1. The Commander has a documented spawn pattern that's exploitable. Plant 43 reappears with new attack patterns — counter strategy differs from chapter 3.

Chapter 8: ARK Core + Eveline (target 20:00)

Final boss Eveline has 4 phases. Frame-perfect counters on phases 3 and 4 are essential. Specific charm: Phoenix Down on phase 4 is meta. The True Ending route requires specific dialogue at the end.

RNG management

Several enemies in RE9 have RNG-dependent attack patterns. Speedrunners use save scumming on Casual; on Hardcore/Insanity, RNG is just part of the run.

  • Vivien Stage 2 attack pattern: 60% RNG-determined. Top runs are roughly 5% better than average.
  • Mr. X chase route variance: ±15s between attempts.
  • Eveline phase transitions: Frame-perfect input timing critical.
