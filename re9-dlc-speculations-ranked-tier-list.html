TL;DR — most likely DLCs

  • Almost certainly: Ada Wong DLC (#1). Beacon cutscene is too specific to be a red herring.
  • Strong probability: Sherry Birkin Italy DLC (#2). Codex mention is unprecedented since RE6.
  • Plausible: Marcus Halverson prequel (#4). Insanity audio framework is ready-made for expansion.
  • Wishful thinking: Multiplayer Survivor (#11). No mainline setup, no leaks.

S-tier — almost certainly happening

#1: Ada Wong DLC

Why #1: The Ada Beacon cutscene (NG+ Insanity unlock) shows Leon hearing Ada's voice on a satellite frequency. She says three words before signal cuts. The setup is too specific to be decoration — Capcom doesn't voice-act 3 sentences and animate a unique cutscene for a one-shot easter egg. Famitsu director Nakanishi specifically refused to confirm "no DLC" when asked about Ada at the May 2026 interview.

Most likely scope: 4-6 hour campaign featuring Ada infiltrating a Connections facility, set 2-3 months after RE9's ending. Could feature young Leon flashback.

See our Ada Wong RE9 reversal arc.

#2: Sherry Birkin Italy DLC

Why S-tier: Leon's personal codex contains: "Last spoke to Sherry 6 months ago. She's in Italy with the BSAA." First named Sherry reference in mainline RE since RE6. Capcom historically doesn't name dead characters off-screen — Sherry is being deliberately positioned for return. Italy location + BSAA setup = sequel hook or DLC.

Most likely scope: 5-7 hour BSAA mission in Italian countryside. Could feature Claire Redfield cameo (TerraSave alumni network).

A-tier — strong setup

#3: Mia Winters narrative DLC

Why A-tier: Mia's helicopter EW-2017 appearance in Grace True Ending is a 3-minute moment. Fans want more closure on her arc post-RE7. EW initials + 2017 year = Ethan Winters tribute. DLC could explore how she became EW-2017 operator.

#4: Marcus Halverson ARK prequel

Why A-tier: Insanity-only family audio logs about Marcus (Chunk's pre-transformation identity) are framework-ready for expansion. Capcom could build 4-6 hour DLC showing Marcus's 14-month transformation timeline — from researcher to monster. Tragic narrative arc Capcom has historically loved (Albert Wesker, Alex Wesker).

See our Chunk origin guide.

#5: Chris Redfield BSAA mission

Why A-tier: Brief Leon codex reference to Chris's recent BSAA assignment. Could pair with #2 (Sherry Italy) as connected DLCs. Chris has been background since RE8 ending — fans want him back in mainline narrative.

B-tier — plausible

#6-8: Setup-supported but not guaranteed

Vivien Ashcroft 2009 DLC — explore her time at The Connections with Eveline. Narrative gap exists.
Eveline Bulgaria photo DLC — direct visual hint in Mia cameo. Could be tie to RE10.
Free-roam Raccoon City — streets are pre-rendered. Wouldn't require new content, just unlock mode.

C-tier — Capcom pattern

#9-10: Franchise-typical content

Mercenaries mode — Capcom's default DLC pattern since RE4. Datamined assets suggest groundwork.
Boss Rush mode — enemy pool framework supports this. Easy implementation.

D-tier — wishful thinking

#11-12: Unlikely but possible

Multiplayer Survivor mode — Capcom hasn't shown interest. No leaks. No setup.
Costume / cosmetic DLC — inevitable but minor. Doesn't count as "real" DLC.

FAQ — RE9 DLC speculations

Q: Has Capcom officially confirmed any DLC?
A: No. May 2026 Famitsu interview was the closest — Nakanishi refused to deny Ada Wong DLC, which the fan community interprets as soft confirmation.

Q: How much do RE DLCs typically cost?
A: $15-30 for story DLC. $5-10 for cosmetic packs. RE9 will likely follow this pattern.

Q: Will RE9 DLC require NG+ completion?
A: Historically yes — RE8 Shadows of Rose required main campaign clear. RE9 DLC likely same.

Q: Are there datamined unused assets?
A: Limited findings. Some unused character animations exist (possibly Ada). Voice file references to "Italy" and "Bulgaria" found in code strings.

Q: Could DLC affect base game endings?
A: Likely no — RE's DLC pattern keeps base game canon untouched. DLC adds new perspectives, not retroactive changes.

Q: Which DLC would fans most want?
A: Ada Wong by a massive margin. Reddit polls consistently show 60-70% prefer Ada DLC over any alternative.

Q: Will any DLC be free?
A: Mercenaries / Boss Rush modes are typically free updates. Story DLC is always paid.

Q: When will Capcom officially announce DLC?
A: Predicted late 2026 (Summer Game Fest 2026 or Tokyo Game Show 2026). Capcom historically announces DLC 6-12 months before release.
