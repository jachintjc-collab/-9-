10개 질문 답변

Q: Best Insanity build for beginners?
A: Tank (W870 + Iron Skin). Forgiving for new players.
Q: Stealth build — viable on Insanity?
A: Yes. Silencer 9 + Whisper charm. Avoid most fights.
Q: Crit build — what charms?
A: Marksman's Eye + Knife Edge + Magnetic Hand.
Q: Speedrun-optimal loadout?
A: Quickstep + Marksman's Eye + Phoenix Down.
Q: Knife-only run — required charms?
A: Mortal Edge from Ch5 + Knife Edge + Quickstep.
Q: NG+ Rocket Launcher build?
A: Equipped from Chapter 1. Crafter's Touch saves ammo.
Q: Charm slot order — does it matter?
A: No. All 4 active simultaneously.
Q: Best build for first playthrough?
A: Don't optimize. Use Silencer 9 + Iron Skin to learn.
Q: Build that requires least skill?
A: Tank — high survival, low precision needed.
Q: Build for Insanity NG+ Hardcore?
A: Crit-Stealth hybrid: Predator + Marksman's Eye + Phoenix.
