DMCA takedown requests

If you are a copyright holder and believe content on this site infringes your rights, please submit a DMCA takedown notice. Per 17 U.S.C. § 512(c), notices must include:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
  3. Identification of the allegedly infringing material with sufficient detail to locate it (URL preferred)
  4. Contact information (address, phone, email)
  5. A statement of good faith belief that the use is unauthorized
  6. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate and the submitter is authorized to act

Counter-notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification including:

  • Your physical or electronic signature
  • Identification of the removed material and its previous location
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the removal was a mistake or misidentification
  • Your name, address, and phone number
  • Consent to jurisdiction of federal court in your district

Submit counter-notifications to the same DMCA email above.

Repeat infringer policy

In accordance with the DMCA, this site terminates access for repeat infringers in appropriate circumstances. Since this site does not host user-uploaded content, this policy primarily applies to any third-party contributors that may be added in the future.

References to Capcom's trademarks, character names, location names, and game mechanics on this site are made for the purposes of:

  • Helping players understand and enjoy the game
  • Providing factual information about gameplay

We believe such use constitutes fair use under U.S. copyright law and applicable international copyright treaties. We do not host or distribute copyrighted artwork, screenshots, audio, or video assets from the game.
