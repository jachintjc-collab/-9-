ARK Explorer — visit every named room in the ARK facility

ARK Explorer requires visiting all 31 named rooms in the chapter 7-8 ARK facility. The named rooms appear on the ARK map system once entered. The trophy ticks at room 31's first entry.

My take:ARK Explorer is the most exploration-rewarding trophy in RE9. The named rooms include several optional areas with significant lore drops. Worth doing for the codex content alone.

The named rooms by floor

Surface level (5 rooms): Entry vestibule, Security checkpoint, Reception hall, Briefing room, Personnel records.

Sub-level 1 (8 rooms): Administrative wing, Server farm, Communications hub, Document storage, three storage rooms, plus the BSL-2 transition airlock.

Sub-level 2 (7 rooms): BSL-3 lab suite, two pathogen sample storage rooms, Mia Winters cryostorage (the chapter 7 reveal location), Plant 43 research lab, Las Plagas research lab, Tyrant containment.

Sub-level 3 (6 rooms): BSL-4 critical containment, three Eveline mold network rooms, primary reactor control, Commander X1 quarters.

Core (5 rooms): ARK central chamber, final boss arena, three peripheral evacuation routes.

The three most-missed rooms

Commander X1 quarters (sub-level 3). Off the main corridor, requires you to investigate a wall panel that doesn't look interactive. The room contains his personal effects and a journal that explains his integration motivation.

Personnel records (surface level). The room is in plain sight but most players run past it during the chapter 7 opening sequence. The records inside contain Sherry Birkin's BSAA personnel file (chapter 6 callback).

Plant 43 research lab (sub-level 2). The door is locked from the chapter 7 entry direction. Requires a specific keycard from sub-level 1's administrative wing. Skipping the keycard skips the room.

Insanity wrinkle

Three rooms have respawning enemies on Insanity that don't appear on Standard. The Plant 43 research lab specifically has two Care Center Hybrids that aren't there on Standard. Bring ammo and Charm slot for Endurance.

Korean fan community room order

Korean speedrun community on Naver documented an optimal room visit order for the trophy. The route avoids backtracking by visiting peripheral rooms before central rooms. Saves roughly 8 minutes compared to wiki-standard ordering.

My take

The ARK Explorer trophy rewards careful exploration. Many of the named rooms contain lore drops that explain the franchise's institutional plot. Pursue this trophy for the lore content, not just the trophy unlock.
