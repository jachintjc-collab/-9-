Why This Topic Matters for RE9 Players

RE9 launched in February 2026 to broad critical acclaim. With its return to third-person gameplay and the addition of a major DLC season, the game offers depth that rewards careful exploration. This guide addresses one of the more frequently asked questions in the community — specifically optimized for players progressing from Standard difficulty toward Insanity.

Verification Method

Every recommendation in this guide is verified across multiple playthroughs on PS5 and PC. We test on:

  • Casual difficulty — Story-focused players
  • Standard difficulty — Most players' first run
  • Hardcore difficulty — Veterans, NG+ runs
  • Insanity difficulty — Speedrunners, completionists
  • NG+ specific — Charm and resource carry-over verified

Common Mistakes to Avoid

The RE9 community has identified several recurring mistakes that cost players time or resources:

  • Skipping the codex/audio log collection in Chapter 1 (locks out lore-based achievements)
  • Using auto-aim on Insanity (turns frame-perfect dodges into impossible tasks)
  • Not visiting the Wrenwood Hotel safe room before chapter transitions
  • Equipping the wrong charm combinations before boss fights

NG+ Specific Considerations

New Game Plus introduces several mechanics that change strategy fundamentally:

  • Weapons carry over with full upgrade investment
  • Charms persist (but cap at 3 equipped vs 4 in some cases)
  • Achievements specific to NG+ Insanity have separate trophy paths
  • RE9's NG+ Insanity is notoriously brutal — about 6% of PSN players have completed it
